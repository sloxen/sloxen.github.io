Mission

Your data and Hybrid Intelligence advisor

Sloxen® helps individuals, small and medium-sized organisations turn messy business data into confident decisions with Hybrid Intelligence Systems (HISx™): end-to-end systems that capture, structure, and analyse data to generate clear and explainable predictions.

Deliberate intelligence for reliable decisions.

Philosophy

Sloths are often mistaken for slow.

In reality, they are efficient and deliberate - evolved to operate in complex environments with minimal waste.

That idea stayed with us. Sloxen® design AI systems that ignore noise, conserve effort, and focus on what matters.

This approach is shaped by research experience in Cambridge and applied in real-world settings, where decisions need to be reliable, not just fast.

The result is intelligence that works under pressure - deliberate, dependable, and easier to trust when decisions count.

Approach

Real-world organisations work with imperfect data and limited resources.

HISx™: combining raw data, machine learning, domain knowledge, and human judgment, to support small and medium-sized organisations, charitable and voluntary groups, resource-limited teams, and individuals working in constrained environments.

This produces practical systems that strengthen human judgement: models surface patterns and risks, while people apply context, values, and accountability - enabling confident decisions without black boxes.

Pricing

We price work based on scope, data complexity, and the decision you need to make.

Our default is small, time-boxed work first - then scale when it is proving value.

How we work

We do not sell fixed packages. We define the right level of support for the work required. Most engagements fall into three levels:

Full HISx™ System

Complete decision system

A tailored HISx™ system that connects your data, workflows, and outputs into reusable materials.

Best for repeat decisions.

Targeted Build

Partial system

A focused build for one product or decision problem. Faster to deliver, with clear outputs for immediate use.

Best for specific use cases.

Rapid Insight Report

Fast analysis

A quick review of your data to produce a clear, practical report with our existing products.

Best when you need clarity quickly.
