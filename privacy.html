Privacy Terms

Privacy

Sloxen® respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal and business data you share with us. This notice explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable data protection law.

Who we are

Sloxen® is a trading name of Sloxen Ltd (Company No. 16907507), a UK-registered data and AI consultancy. If you have any questions about this notice or how we handle data, please fill out the contact form or email us

What data we collect

Depending on the nature of your enquiry or engagement, we may collect and process:

  • Personal contact details (such as name and email address)
  • Business information provided during enquiries or projects
  • Raw data and metadata relating to clients’ small and medium-sized business operations, systems, or datasets
  • Messages and supporting context shared with us

How we use your data

We use data only to:

  • Respond to enquiries and communications
  • Deliver agreed consultancy, analytical, or technical services
  • Support analysis, modelling, automation, and decision-making as requested by clients

We do not use client data for marketing or model training purposes without explicit consent.

How client data is processed

Client raw data and metadata are processed only as necessary to provide the relevant products or services. Communications and data exchange are handled via Sloxen® official email address suffixed with sloxen.com only. The specific email address prefix used depends on the products or services being provided, for example, "info@", "data@", or "recruit@" etc.

Third-party services

Messages submitted through our website contact form are processed using a third-party form service Formspree and delivered to our email inbox. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data retention

We keep personal and business data only for as long as necessary to respond to an enquiry, deliver services, or meet legal, regulatory, or contractual requirements.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR and related data protection laws, you have the right to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data in certain circumstances. To exercise these rights, please fill out the contact form or email us

Cookies

Sloxen Ltd does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies on this website.

Changes to this notice

We may update this privacy notice from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. The latest version will always be available on this page.
